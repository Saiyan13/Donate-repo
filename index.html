Our Donate Plan

We already dispatched 400,000 water purification tablets that can support 80,000 households with clean water for a week. We are working to further support the Government of Bangladesh's emergency response with millions of water purification tablets, more than 10,000 water containers known as jerry cans, and thousands of hygiene kits for women and adolescent girls. We are also procuring emergency medicines supplies for district health facilities.
